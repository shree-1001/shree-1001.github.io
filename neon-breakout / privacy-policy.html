PRIVACY POLICY
Effective Date: June 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Neon Breakout ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile game application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Neon Breakout is an offline game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

The only data stored by the app is:

  • Your high scores and player name — stored locally on your device only
  • Your game progress (levels unlocked) — stored locally on your device only

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Neon Breakout displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage data

This data is collected by Google, not by us. Google's use of advertising data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Go to Settings → Google → Ads
  • Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

3. Children's Privacy

Neon Breakout is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, the app is safe for children to use.

AdMob has been configured to serve ads appropriate for general audiences.

4. Data Security

Since Neon Breakout does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no personal data at risk. Your high scores are stored only on your local device and are protected by your device's own security.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

GOOGLE ADMOB — Advertising service by Google LLC

6. Permissions

Neon Breakout may request the following permissions:

  • Internet Access — Required to load advertisements from Google AdMob
  • Network State — Used to check internet connectivity for ads

No other permissions are required or requested.
